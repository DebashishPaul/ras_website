Mission

Keeping in line with the parent body of IEEE RAS, the mission IEEE NSU SB RAS is to –“ facilitate the exchange of scientific and technological knowledge in Robotics and Automation that benefits members, the profession and humanity.” IEEE NSU RAS also aims to maintain good academic standards in research and other literary forms relating this field.

Vision

The vision of IEEE NSU SB RAS is to make this student chapter a shining beacon of quality, creativity, academic integrity and innovation. From the very beginning IEEE NSU SB RAS has been encouraging the dissipation of robotics and automation related knowledge within the engineering student body of North South University. Since the beginning of IEEE NSU RAS, Its members have been actively and enthusiastically bring into realization workshops on robotics.
